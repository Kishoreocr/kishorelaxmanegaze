How do landlords benefit from property monitoring services?

When people invest in property, they secure a certain future, a certain return. While they are aware of the maintenance costs and legal formalities, it becomes challenging if the property is located hundreds of miles away. There are many landlords in India and abroad who have bought properties in suburban areas and rural areas as their second homes or holiday homes. They may have other objectives such as investment for future occupation or for any other purpose

Landlords also need to deal with existing state laws on occupancy or vacancy of a property along with pending taxes, bills or penalties. Here’s where property monitoring services come into picture. Since most landlords are not experts of property taxes and laws, it’s best if they hire professionals who are updated and well aware of the existing laws and norms.

Here’s how landlords can benefit from property monitoring services -

Lower maintenance and repair costs

Veteran landlords are aware the kind of problems legal hassles can cost both financially and personally. A good property monitoring service company is armed with legal knowledge of the latest property occupation or vacancy laws. For instance, in India, vacant properties are taxed in order to motivate landlords to put them up for rent. These taxes and laws need knowledge and monitoring. Property monitoring companies help landlords with regulations and compliances easily since they are dealing in them on a regular basis.

Filling the legal gaps

The Modi Government focused on affordable housing during its tenure that helped encouraged developers in this segment. This saw larger players such as Mahindra Lifespace, Tata Housing, Puravankara Projects or Shapoorji Pallonji Group entering this segment. In fact, Emgee Group announced an investment of Rs 1,600 crore into the affordable housing segment in the next five years. This has given confidence to private equity players to partner with developers operating in this specific niche. PE firms such as Essel Finance Advisors, Brick Eagle Capital, Avenue Venture Partners Real Estate, Nisus Finance Services have launched such partnerships. Regardless of the change in the government, this has set off a trend in the housing sector which looks likely to continue.

Increasing the value of the investment

One of the biggest benefits of preventive maintenance is that it helps increase the value of any property. This means they need proper systems at place that may catch any repair issues. Just installing IoT devices is not enough today. It’s important that personalized visits by a professionals are conducted in order to cross-check the functioning of existing systems. This not only prevents any hazards but also contributes in elevating the value of the property. Landlords should hire property monitoring companies that keep this as their leading objective.

Peace of mind

The assurance that your property is not abandoned or misused leads to peace of mind. Properties that are located in rural or suburban areas are prone to encroachment or misuse by local people. But when you hire a property monitoring service that pays visits to your property regularly, it creates an unsaid sense of protection around your property.

However, it’s vital to choose a professional property monitoring service company that specializes in Indian laws along with compliances of maintenance.
