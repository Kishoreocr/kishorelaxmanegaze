About eGAZE

Headquartered in Hyderabad, eGAZE is a fast-growing property monitoring service company that seeks to empower property owners living in India and abroad. We employ a complete software solution to make it convenient for people living in any part of the world to monitor properties without physically visiting our offices.

The eGAZE Way is our protocol, our Code of Conduct. It truly reflects our value system and beliefs. For us, someone’s home, farm house or any property represents their personality, their values. We treat properties the way we treat people. And these values are embedded deeply in our style of working and environment.

Here’s what The eGAZE Way really means to us -



Be Transparent

Our clients are human beings first. We feel their anxiety, their stress about properties that are so far away from them. We keep an open communication channel that keeps our clients at peace.


Be good listeners

For us a client request or complaint is not a support ticket, it’s a genuine concern. We truly listen to our clients with empathy and that helps us deliver according to their preferences.


Be Accountable

While reports are an integral part of our work, we don’t work for reports. We work to monitor, protect and guard properties. Whether we hit or we miss, we take full responsibility for what we do and how we do it.


Be resourceful

We began property monitoring service with full confidence in our resources – legal, personal and professional. Our team is well aware of property trends, laws & compliances and the changing needs of the market.
